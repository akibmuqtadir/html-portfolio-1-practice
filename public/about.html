About Me

Beyond academics, I enjoy gaming, watching movies, and self-studying tech topics. I’m currently exploring research opportunities that combine environmental science and data-driven solutions. With a goal to develop practical skills in AI and analytics, I aim to contribute to sustainable, data-informed innovations for the future.
